# Outfit-Curator-Kiosk
Here are some ideas that our mall kiosk can do (tentative): 
* Have access to real-time inventory from all stores in the mall to let customer knows what available in person stores. 
* Help customer generate complement clothes based on what they already help and/or help customer generate a whole outfit (top, bottom, accessories) based on customer-selected criteria (occasion, size, gender, color preference).
* Price breakdown and showing deals/ ongoing discount of stores within that month.
* Print out kiosk "receipt", including: itemID and item names, store locations (floor and suite number), prices of individual items, total cost.
